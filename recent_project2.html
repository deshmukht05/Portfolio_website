Overview

Bajaoo is a user-friendly e-commerce app designed for buying and selling musical instruments. My Figma UI design covers the complete user journey, from the Login/Signup page to the Payment Gateway, ensuring a seamless shopping experience. The design appears to focus on clean aesthetics, intuitive navigation, and smooth checkout flow, catering to musicians and music enthusiasts.

Key Featuers

  • Authentication & Onboarding:
    • - Login/Signup Page: Clean and minimal design with email/phone and social login options.
    - OTP Verification: Secure and fast authentication flow.
    - Welcome/Onboarding Screens: Introduces app features for new users.
  • Home & Product Discovery:
    • - Search Bar: Allows users to quickly find instruments.
    - Categories Section: Organized by instrument types (Guitars, Pianos, Drums, etc.).
    - Featured Products & Deals: Highlighted with attractive banners.
    - Trending & Recommendations: Personalized suggestions based on user preferences.
  • Product Pages:
    • - High-Quality Images & Zoom Feature: Lets users inspect instruments closely.
    - Detailed Specifications: Includes brand, model, material, and technical details.
    - Customer Reviews & Ratings: Builds trust and helps in decision-making.
    - "Add to Cart" & "Buy Now" Buttons: Clear CTAs for quick purchases.
  • Cart & Checkout Flow:
    • - Cart Summary: Displays selected items, prices, and total cost.
    - Coupon/Discount Field: Encourages users to apply promo codes.
    - Shipping & Delivery Options: Multiple choices (express, standard).
    - Secure Payment Gateway: Supports UPI, Cards, Net Banking, and Wallet payments.
